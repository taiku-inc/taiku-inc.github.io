Verbal Consent Script for Toll-Free SMS Opt-In

Taiku, Inc.

1. Overview

This document outlines the official script to be used by Taiku, Inc. ("Taiku AI") employees or contractors to obtain verbal consent for SMS messaging.

  • Intended Use: This script must be read verbatim during a live phone interaction when requesting SMS opt-in permission.
  • Compliance: All instances of verbal consent must be recorded and securely stored, or accurately logged with a timestamp, to maintain a clear audit trail.

2. Use Case Details

  • Use Case Category: Customer care
  • Message Frequency: Approximately 2 to 5 messages per month
  • Opt-Out Command: STOP
  • Help Command: HELP

3. Verbal Consent Script

(Begin Script)

Agent: "Thank you for choosing Taiku AI. To help you stay up-to-date with your account and receive responses to your customer support inquiries, we would like to be able to send you text messages.

You may receive approximately 2 to 5 messages per month depending on your inquiries. Please be aware that message and data rates may apply.

You are always in control of your subscription. You can reply with the word HELP at any time for assistance, or reply with the word STOP to cancel and opt out of all future messages.

Do we have your consent to send you these informational text messages from Taiku, Inc.? Please say 'Yes' to opt-in or 'No' to decline."

Customer: "Yes."

Agent: "Great, thank you. We have noted your consent. We will now send a confirmation text message to your mobile number to confirm your enrollment. Thank you for connecting with Taiku AI!"

(End Script)

4. Required Follow-up Confirmation SMS

Upon receiving verbal consent, the following one-time confirmation message must be sent to the user's phone number immediately.

Message to be sent:

"Taiku AI Alerts: You have successfully subscribed to receive account messages. Msg freq varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel."
